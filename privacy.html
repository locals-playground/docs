Privacy Policy

App Name: InSight Laax
Last Updated: January 2026

Your privacy is important to us. Ideally, we want to know as little about you as possible.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

2. Push Notifications

To send you lift status alerts, we generate an anonymous device token via Apple Push Notification Services (APNs). This token allows us to send messages to your device but is not linked to your personal identity.

3. Local Storage

Your preferences (e.g., your list of subscribed lifts) are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage mechanisms. We do not have access to this list on our servers.

4. Third Parties

We do not sell or share any data with third parties. The app fetches public lift data directly from the LAAX API.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at: hello@localsplayground.ch
